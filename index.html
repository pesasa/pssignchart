Pssignchart examples

Here are couple of examples, how to use pssignchart.js.

<div class="pssc1"> </div> <script type="text/javascript"> jQuery('.pssc1').psnumberline(); jQuery('.pssc1').pssignchart('add', {func: '2x-3', roots:[{label:'\\frac{3}{2}', value:1.5}]}); jQuery('.pssc1').pssignchart('add', {func: 'x^2', roots:[0]}); jQuery('.pssc1').pssignchart('add', {func: 'x-2', roots: [2]}); jQuery('.pssc1').pssignchart('add', {func: 'x^2-1', roots: [-1, 1]}); jQuery('.pssc1').pssignchart('total', {func: '\\frac{\\left(x^2\\right)\\left(x-2\\right)}{x^2-1}'}); </script>
